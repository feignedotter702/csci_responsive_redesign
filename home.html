The Room Official Site

This is the official website of a movie website. It was released in 2003 and is mainly used to post information about the movie release, production, screening ceremony, and the sale of movie peripherals. Every few years, information about the film's tour will be posted on this site.

I redesigned the The Room official web page to be responsive across various device sizes while improving the site’s usability and visual design.

Current Issues

Usability

Performing a heuristic evaluation and analyzing the site based on criteria of usability, learnability, and memorability revealed many issues with the page’s visual design and lack of responsiveness.

Accessibility

When using a screen reader to assess the site’s accessibility for people who are visually impaired, the lack of alternative titles made it impossible to understand images. The use of empty headings and lack of hierarchy between headings and text also made comprehension difficult.
